Easy Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread recipe is quick and easy! There are no hard to get ingredients and can be done in 1 bowl.

It is also very easy to add your own spin to it. For example, you can add some cinammon or chocolate sprinkles on top.

Ingredients

  • 205g flour
  • 76g melted butter
  • 1 Beaten egg
  • 150g sugar
  • 1 packet of baking soda
  • salt to taste
  • 3 Ripe bananas
  • 1tsp Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mash the bananas
  2. Mix in the melted butter
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt
  4. Add the egg, sugar and vanilla extract
  5. Mix in the flour
  6. Bake for 45-55 minutes at 180 degrees Celcius
