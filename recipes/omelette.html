Quick Omelette

Description

Easy Omelette!

Add your preferred ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • butter

Steps

  1. Add butter to the pan
  2. Beat the eggs along with the salt and pepper
  3. Mix in whatever you want(E.g. paprika or onions)
  4. While the pan is on medium heat add the beaten eggs and stir the eggs lightly. Move the pan while stirring to avoid creating empty pockets
  5. When the eggs can not fill the empty pockets stop stirring
  6. Draw a line with your utensil through the middle and fold along it
  7. let it cook for some time more, use your judgement
  8. Serve!
