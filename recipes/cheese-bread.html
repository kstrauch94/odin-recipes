Brazilian Cheese Puffs

Description

Quick and easy brazilian cheese puffs recipe.

Get ready to lick your fingers and gain some weight!

Ingredients

  • 170g flour
  • 160ml milk
  • 60ml Vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 packet of baking soda
  • salt to taste
  • 25g Grana padano or parmesan
  • 85g mozarella

Steps

  1. prepare the flour, sal and baking powder in a bowl
  2. Bring the milk and oil to a boil
  3. Pour the milk-oil mix into the flour and mix
  4. Add the egg and mix
  5. add the cheese and mix
  6. Spray oil into cupcake tray and fill each to half with the mixture
  7. Bake at 200 degree Celcius for 20 minutes
